Hammer Bridge Path

One Tap. One Perfect Bridge.

⚒️ Build. Smash. Conquer Every Gap! ⚒️

Hammer Bridge Path is an exciting arcade adventure where you build powerful hammers to bridge gaps and crush monster faces! Tap and hold to stretch your hammer across platforms — but make sure it's just the right length!

Smash enemies, collect points, and test your timing and precision as you travel through a path full of danger and destruction. Can you master the perfect strike and become the ultimate gap-crossing crusher?

How to Play

  1. Tap and hold to grow your hammer — the longer you hold, the longer it stretches
  2. Release at the perfect moment to bridge the gap between platforms
  3. Too short and you'll fall; too long and you'll overshoot — precision is key!
  4. Smash through monster faces blocking your path for bonus points
  5. Keep advancing through increasingly challenging levels and obstacles
