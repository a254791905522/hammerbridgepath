Privacy Policy

Hammer Bridge Path

Effective Date: March 24, 2026
🔒
Zero Data Collection Hammer Bridge Path does not collect any personal data from its users. Your smashing adventure remains completely private.

📜 About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Hammer Bridge Path mobile application ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to being transparent about our data practices. This document explains what information we do and do not collect when you use our app to build hammer bridges, crush monsters, and conquer gaps. Privacy is fundamental to our design philosophy, and we built Hammer Bridge Path with that principle at its core.

🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be absolutely clear: Hammer Bridge Path does not collect, process, transmit, or store any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

🙅 Identity Info No names, emails, or phone numbers
📡 Location No GPS or IP-based tracking
📈 Analytics No usage patterns or session logs
📱 Device Data No hardware IDs or fingerprints
💳 Financial No payment or billing details
🌐 Network No browsing history or cookies

✅ Your Privacy Commitments

  • No account registration or sign-in is required to enjoy the game
  • No third-party analytics frameworks or tracking SDKs are embedded
  • No behavioral advertising or ad-tracking networks are used
  • All game progress and settings are stored exclusively on your local device
  • No data of any kind is transmitted to external servers
  • No social media integrations that could share your activity

💾 On-Device Storage

Hammer Bridge Path may save game-related preferences and progress data locally on your device to enhance your gameplay experience. This data is stored using standard iOS storage mechanisms, never leaves your device, and cannot be accessed by us or any third party. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

👾 Third-Party Services

Hammer Bridge Path operates as a fully self-contained application. We do not integrate any third-party SDKs, services, or frameworks that collect user data. There are no external analytics platforms, no embedded advertising networks, and no social media plugins within the app.

👨‍👩‍👦 Age Considerations

Hammer Bridge Path is rated 13+ and contains infrequent cartoon or fantasy violence. Since we collect absolutely no personal data, the app maintains a strong privacy posture for users of all ages. We do not knowingly target or collect information from children under 13.

⚠️

We recommend that parents and guardians monitor their children's use of all mobile applications and review relevant privacy policies. While Hammer Bridge Path collects no data, responsible digital habits start with awareness.

🔄 Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time to reflect updates in our practices or changes required by applicable law. When changes are made, the revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
